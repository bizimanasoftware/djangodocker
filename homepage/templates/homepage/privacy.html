Gloex.org Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 16, 2025

Your privacy is important to us at Gloex.org. This Privacy Policy explains what limited information we collect, how we use it, and the minimal extent to which we might share it.

1. Data Collection

We are committed to a minimal data collection approach.

1.1 Information We Collect

When you register for an account on Gloex.org, we collect only the most basic information necessary for your account creation and management:

  • Username: To identify your account on the Platform.
  • Personal Email Address: To communicate with you regarding your account and important Platform updates.

1.2 Anonymity for Sponsors/Donors

To protect the privacy of those offering support, the identities of Sponsors and donors are kept anonymous on the public platform.

2. Purpose of Data Collection

The limited data we collect serves specific purposes:

  • Account Management: Your username and email are used to create, maintain, and manage your user account on Gloex.org.
  • Communication: Your email address allows us to send you essential communications, such as account verification emails, password reset links, important updates regarding Platform changes, and occasional newsletters related to Gloex.org's activities.

3. Data Sharing

We have a strict policy regarding your personal data.

3.1 No Sharing

We do not sell, rent, lease, or otherwise share your personal data (username and email) with third parties for their marketing purposes.

3.2 Exceptions

We may disclose your personal data only if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court order, government request).

4. User Data Control

You have control over the information you choose to make public.

4.1 Public Profiles

Any information you choose to make public on your Gloex.org profile, such as your talent description, aid request, financial addresses (wallet, bank, mobile money details), pictures, or other personal details, is done so at your sole discretion and responsibility.

  • Once you choose to make this information public, it is no longer governed by this Privacy Policy regarding its public visibility.
  • Gloex.org is not responsible for how other users or third parties collect, use, or share the information you make publicly available.

5. Security Measures

While we do not handle or store sensitive financial data, we take reasonable measures to protect the limited personal data we do hold (your username and email).

  • We implement general security practices, including using secure servers and industry-standard data encryption for data in transit, to protect against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction of your username and email.

6. Disclaimers

6.1 Sensitive Information

As stated in our Terms of Service, we strongly advise you not to post any sensitive financial information (e.g., passwords, bank routing numbers, credit/debit card PINs or CVC codes) or highly sensitive personal addresses (like your home address if you don't want it publicly known) on your public profile or share it with others through any communication channel.

  • Gloex.org is not responsible for any security breaches, financial losses, or other damages that may result from your decision to publicly share such sensitive information or to send it to others, even those claiming to be legitimate Sponsors. You do so entirely at your own risk.

By using Gloex.org, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.
